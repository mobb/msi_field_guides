You can explore kelp forests by snorkeling and SCUBA diving at many places along the California coast. Charter operations in Santa Barbara and Ventura offer diving and snorkeling trips to the Channel Islands. You can also explore kelp forests without even getting wet at the REEF and OCTOS at UCSB's Marine Science Institute, as well as many other wonderful public aquariums in California such as those listed below:
